My work

Our first assignment was to code a website from scratch.

In this Group project, each of us had to code a semantically correct page with relevant content and images, and also had to create an edited image in Photoshop.

During this period I was introduced to Photoshop, HTML and CSS.

What I did

First of all, I created some sketches based on how I would want the website to look like and gathered ideas about what the photoshopped picture should be.

After the brainstorming, we all met and shared our ideas, then collectively agreed on the idea and the style everyone liked.

We started coding our pages individually. I edited the picture in Photoshop. Our website was about a facility in space, so I decided to edit a picture of a bar, that has a view on space. I altered a picture of the moon in space, cut out the cafeteria windows and inserted my picture of space
