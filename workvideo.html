My work

The second assignment was to make a so called "How to" video.

Here, I acquired knowledge about techincal terms, video genres and compositions, and also learned basic editing, animation and color grading in Adobe Premiere and After Effects.

Our job was to come up with an idea that shows an action process. It was hard to agree on one topic, but in the end we chose to make a video on "How to frame a picture", which is kind of a play of words.

What I did

As soon as we decided on the idea, we started shooting. The idea with the title "How to frame a picture" was that we are also framing the picture for murder.

We decided to make the video in a stop motion style. Our group gathered when everyone was free and we cut out paper parts that we would use for the video and assembled them.

I was operating the camera, and managing the flow of the project. I also edited some parts together and did the outro image.
